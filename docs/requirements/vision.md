# Product Vision

## Overview

MindVault is a personal productivity platform designed to centralize important information in a single environment, acting as a user's digital second brain.

## Mission

Help users organize their lives by providing tools for managing habits, finances, goals, notes, projects, studies, reminders, and personal productivity.

## Target Audience

People who want to centralize their personal organization in a secure and intuitive platform.

## Core Values

- Simplicity
- Productivity
- Security
- Organization
- Personal growth
